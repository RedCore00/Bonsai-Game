Balkon-Bonsai

A calm bonsai sandbox: start tiny, bend it into shape, prune specific branches, keep it healthy, and build a photo album.

How it works
• Start with a tiny plant.
• Bend is the main way to gain “Form”.
• Prune cuts the branch you click on (exact position).
• Water uses the can animation and restores water instantly.
• Keep water inside the ideal range to maintain health.
Controls
Tools: W water · P prune · B bend · Esc cancel

Bend: drag trunk handles or a branch tip.
Prune: click a branch segment where you want to cut.
Quit just returns to this menu. To close the game, close the browser tab/window.
